## Overview

A live library page for TASIS Portugal, oriented around what people actually come to the page to find: hours, services, resources, and how to ask for help. The redesign keeps the sense of place that makes a school library worth visiting while putting the practical information first.

## My role

UX/UI design and content structure.

## Approach

- Lead with practical information: hours, where to go, how to ask for help.
- Treat collections and services as scannable lists rather than paragraphs.
- Let photography and atmosphere support the page rather than lead it.

## Outcome

A library page that respects both jobs at once, informational and emotional, without sacrificing one for the other.

The page is live at [tasisportugal.org/academics/libraries](https://www.tasisportugal.org/academics/libraries).
