## Overview

A resource hub for admissions agents: the people who introduce QMS to prospective families abroad. Agents need accurate, current information in a form that's easy to scan in conversations and easy for the marketing team to maintain.

This page is a placeholder. The project is in development, and the final case study will replace this content when the work is ready to share publicly.

## What this case study will cover

- The agent's information needs, and how those differ from a public admissions page
- Information architecture and content hierarchy decisions
- A maintainable WordPress content model
- How the design protects against drift between agent-facing and public information
