## Overview

A homepage redesign concept for TASIS Portugal, focused on what a school’s homepage needs to do first: introduce the school clearly, signal trust, and guide prospective families toward a next step.

## My role

UX/UI design. Hierarchy, page structure, and visual treatment.

## Approach

- Anchored the page around one primary admissions action and a small set of supporting ones.
- Built a hierarchy that resolves cleanly on mobile first.
- Concentrated trust signals near decision points rather than scattering them across the page.

## Outcome

Presented as a concept piece. The work demonstrates an approach to marketing-site hierarchy and admissions pathways appropriate for an independent school.

The live concept page is at [tasisportugal.org/home-clone-two](https://www.tasisportugal.org/home-clone-two).
